1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website and services:

  • Contact Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and business details when you submit forms or contact us.
  • Service-Related Information: Details about your business needs, preferences, and any information you share in messages or forms.
  • Usage and Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring URLs collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communication Data: Records of communications with you, including email, phone, SMS/MMS, and other interactions related to our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services: Including website development, app development, digital marketing, and related consulting.
  • To respond to inquiries: To communicate with you about your requests, consultations, and projects.
  • To send marketing and informational communications: Including email and, where permitted, SMS/MMS messages relating to our services, offers, and updates.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure our website: Including analytics, troubleshooting, and protecting against fraud or misuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Such as record-keeping, tax, and regulatory requirements.
  • Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

3. SMS / MMS Messaging Policy

By providing your mobile phone number and choosing to receive communications from us, you may opt in to receive SMS and/or MMS messages from TGAND Technologies LLC related to our services, consultations, promotions, and updates.

Message Content & Frequency

  • Messages may include appointment reminders, service updates, promotional offers, and other information related to TGAND Tech services.
  • Message frequency may vary depending on your interactions with us and the services you receive.

Opt-In

  • You opt in to receive SMS/MMS communications by explicitly providing your phone number through our website forms or other channels and indicating your consent.
  • Consent to receive SMS/MMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

Opt-Out

  • You may opt out of SMS/MMS messages at any time by replying "STOP" to any message you receive from us.
  • After you send "STOP," we may send a confirmation message to verify that you have been unsubscribed. No further marketing SMS/MMS messages will be sent to your number unless you opt in again.

Help & Support

  • For assistance regarding our SMS/MMS program, you can reply "HELP" to any message or contact us at David@TGANDTechnologies.com.

Message & Data Rates

  • Standard message and data rates may apply for any SMS/MMS messages sent or received, depending on your mobile carrier plan.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, understand how visitors use the site, and improve our services.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
